Healthcare Data Schema
Table Relationships
  • One account to many charges via invoice_id
  • One account to many transactions via invoice_id
  • Transactions to crosswalk hw_trn_codes on txn_sub_type_code
Key Business Rules
  • Balance Formula: Balance = Total Charges - Total Payments - Total Adjustments
  • Account Balance: Account Balance = Insurance Balance + Patient Balance
  • AR Status: If balance ≠ 0 then "Open", else "Closed"
  • Date Logic: first_claim_bill_date should be ≤ last_claim_bill_date
